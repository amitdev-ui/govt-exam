Syllabus Highlights

The CET 2025 syllabus is comprehensive and covers four main sections with equal weightage: General Intelligence & Reasoning, General Awareness, Quantitative Aptitude, and English Language & Comprehension. Understanding the detailed syllabus is crucial for efficient preparation.

Overview of CET 2025 Syllabus

The CET 2025 exam consists of four main sections, each carrying equal weightage. The total exam is for 100 marks with multiple-choice questions testing candidates' aptitude across various domains:

Important Exam Details

  • Exam Duration: 60 minutes (1 hour)
  • Total Questions: 100 multiple-choice questions
  • Marking Scheme: +1 for correct answer, no negative marking
  • Medium: Bilingual (Hindi & English)
  • Qualifying Marks: 40% (General), 35% (OBC), 30% (SC/ST)

Verbal Reasoning

  • Syllogisms
  • Statement-Conclusion

Expert Preparation Strategies

A strategic approach to CET preparation can significantly increase your chances of success:

Study Plan Creation
  • Allocate specific time for each subject daily
  • Focus more on your weak areas
  • Keep 1-2 hours for revision every week
  • Set realistic daily and weekly targets
Practice Strategy
  • Solve previous year papers weekly
  • Take timed mock tests regularly
  • Analyze performance after each test
  • Improve speed and accuracy simultaneously
Resource Utilization
  • Use standard books for core concepts
  • Follow reliable newspapers for current affairs
  • Join online test series for practice
  • Create concise notes for quick revision
Final Month Strategy
  • Focus on revision rather than new topics
  • Take at least 15 full-length mock tests
  • Practice time management techniques
  • Stay healthy and maintain positive mindset
Expert Advice

"Focus on understanding the concepts rather than rote memorization. Regular practice and analyzing your mistakes will help improve your performance significantly. The CET exam tests your application skills more than your theoretical knowledge."

- Rahul Sharma, CET Topper
