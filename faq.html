Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries about CET 2025 and the portal.

What is CET?
CET (Common Eligibility Test) is a national-level exam for recruitment to various government jobs in India.
Who conducts the CET?
The National Recruitment Agency (NRA) conducts the CET for Group B and C posts.
How can I apply for CET 2025?
You can apply online through the official CET portal. Visit the 'Apply Now' section for details.
Is there any age limit for CET?
Yes, the age limit varies by category and post. Please refer to the official notification for details.
Can I take the CET in Hindi?
Yes, CET is conducted in multiple languages including Hindi and English.
How long is the CET score valid?
The CET score is valid for 3 years from the date of result declaration.
Where can I find study resources?
You can find study materials, mock tests, and tips in the 'Study Resources' section of this portal.
