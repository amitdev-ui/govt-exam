Important Exam Details

  • Exam Mode: Offline (OMR-based)
  • Duration: 90 minutes
  • Negative Marking: No
  • Language: Bilingual (Hindi & English)

Detailed Syllabus

General Awareness

  • History and Culture of India
  • Indian Polity & Constitution
  • Indian Geography
  • General Science
  • Economic and Social Development

Reasoning Ability

  • Series and Pattern
  • Syllogism
  • Blood Relations
  • Direction Sense
  • Coding-Decoding

Quantitative Aptitude

  • Simplification
  • Data Interpretation
  • Profit and Loss
  • Time, Work, and Distance
  • Ratio & Proportion

Haryana General Knowledge

  • History of Haryana
  • Geography and Districts
  • Culture, Fairs, and Festivals
  • Current Government Schemes

Preparation Tips

Study Strategy (अध्ययन रणनीति)

  • Understand the syllabus and exam structure thoroughly
    पाठ्यक्रम और परीक्षा संरचना को अच्छी तरह से समझें
  • Create a balanced timetable covering each subject daily
    प्रत्येक विषय को दैनिक रूप से कवर करने वाला संतुलित समय सारणी बनाएं
  • Practice with previous year papers and mock tests
    पिछले वर्ष के पेपर और मॉक टेस्ट के साथ अभ्यास करें
  • Focus on Haryana GK as it carries highest weightage
    हरियाणा सामान्य ज्ञान पर ध्यान दें क्योंकि इसमें सबसे अधिक वेटेज है
