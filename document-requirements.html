Complete Document Checklist

Essential Documents:

  • Recent passport size photograph (3.5 x 4.5 cm)
  • Scanned signature (3.5 x 1.5 cm)
  • Valid photo ID proof (Aadhaar Card/Voter ID/Passport)
  • 10th and 12th mark sheets
  • Graduation degree/mark sheet (if applicable)
  • Category certificate (if applicable)
  • Domicile certificate (if applicable)
  • Experience certificate (if applicable)
  • NOC from current employer (if applicable)

Additional Requirements:

  • Disability certificate (for PwD candidates)
  • Income certificate (for EWS candidates)
  • Sports certificate (if applying under sports quota)
  • Ex-serviceman certificate (if applicable)

Note: All documents should be clear, legible, and in the specified format.
