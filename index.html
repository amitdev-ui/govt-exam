About Common Eligibility Test (CET)

The Common Eligibility Test (CET) is a standardized examination conducted by the National Recruitment Agency (NRA) for preliminary screening of candidates for various Group B and C (non-technical) posts in the Government of India.

The CET score remains valid for three years, allowing candidates to apply for multiple government positions without taking separate preliminary exams.
