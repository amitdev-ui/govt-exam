New Reservation Criteria for Group D Posts

Announced: April 15, 2025

The Government of Haryana has introduced new reservation criteria for Group D posts in the upcoming CET 2025. The changes aim to provide more opportunities to candidates from economically weaker sections and ensure balanced representation across different categories.

Key Changes:

  • 10% reservation for Economically Weaker Sections (EWS)
  • 4% reservation for Persons with Benchmark Disabilities (PwBD)
  • 10% reservation for Ex-Servicemen
  • District-wise quota system for certain positions
